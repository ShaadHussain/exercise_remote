
### Jump Rope 15–20 min Routine 

**Warm-up (3–5 min)**

- 30 sec easy jump
    
- 30 sec rest
    
- Repeat 3–4x
    
- Focus on relaxed shoulders, small jumps
    

**Main set (10–12 min)**  
Do **5 rounds**:

- 1 min jump (steady pace)
    
- 30 sec rest
    

Each round, rotate the style:

1. Basic bounce
    
2. Alternate feet
    
3. High knees (controlled, not sprinting)
    
4. Boxer step
    
5. Faster basic bounce
    

**Finisher (2–3 min)**

- 20 sec fast jump
    
- 40 sec slow jump  
    Repeat 3x
